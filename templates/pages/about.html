OUR STORY

Made by 2 Mothers

The story of how two friends set out to change the way children eat, one millet-based meal at a time.

How it all started

We were two mothers searching for healthy eating options for our kids. We visited supermarkets, checked product back-labels, and found that almost everything had trans-fats, high sugar, refined white flour (maida), and synthetic preservatives.

We realized that parents were stuck in a compromise: choosing between convenient junk foods and tedious home preparations. We decided to bring back the nutritional wisdom of our grandmothers (millets like Ragi, Jowar, Bajra) but in formats that kids love and parents find convenient!

Our Journey

2016 — The Inception

Highness was born out of a Delhi home kitchen. We spent months formulating recipes with food scientists, organic farmers, and pediatricians.

2018 — Retail Launch

We hit the shelves in 100+ stores across Delhi and Bangalore. Our colorful, playful animal-themed packaging immediately won kids' hearts.

2021 — Going Global

Expanded our operations to the UK, UAE, and USA, bringing honest, healthy millet foods to families worldwide.

2025 — Millet Revolution

Over 1 Million families served! Named India's most trusted kids nutrition brand.
